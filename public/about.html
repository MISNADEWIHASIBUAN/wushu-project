ABOUT 8thWJWC

8thWJWC LOGO

Represent one of wushu style, boys playing in cudgel style

The shape symbolize of javan rhinoceros, also known as the Sunda rhinoceros or lesser one-horned rhinoceros, is a very rare member of the family Rhinocerotidae and one of five extant rhinoceroses. The Javan Rhino is the rarest of the rhino species with ~75 animals currently surviving only in Indonesia. Javan rhinos are now found only in Indonesia’s Ujung Kulon National Park, Banten Province. The venue for this Championship is also in the province of Banten.

Implicitly, this shape is the number from the eighth championship and also symbolizes eternity, which means to be sustainable and united

The green to yellow gradient color is a color that is adjusted to the color suggestions from the IWUF visual identity guide. It also symbolizes youth, freshness and glory.
